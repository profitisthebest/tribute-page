Robbery by Juice WRLD

The greatest song in existance

Here are some fun facts about this amazing song:

  • This amazing piece of art was released on February 13, 2019. However it was recorded by Juice WRLD in 2018.
  • The song, produced by Nick Mira, features lyrics written by Jarad Higgins, Nick Mira, and Juice WRLD himself.
  • Robbery is noted as a "heartfelt track about a love that has left Juice broken and confused," by the popular hip-hop magazine known as XXL.
  • The official music video was released on Valentine's Day of 2019 to the YouTube channel Lyrical Lemonade, owned by music video director Cole Bennett.
  • The success of the song Robbery was undeniable as it charted on the US Billboard Top 100 as well as the US Rolling Stone top 100.
